Thai Food Encyclopedia

“Ahaan Thai” ( อาหารไทย ) means “Thai food” and is the large, comprehensive encyclopedia for Thai food from A to Z. This interactive reference work offers an alphabetical listing of 697 famous but also lesser-known Thai dishes.

On the detail pages of the individual dishes, you will find linked recipes and further information. You can also display the dishes sorted by Thai region or use the search functionality and tags to find individual dishes.

If you know a good recipe for one of the dishes, please leave a link to it as a comment and I’ll be happy to add it! Or if you miss a dish, feel free to write to me at info@ahaan-thai.de
