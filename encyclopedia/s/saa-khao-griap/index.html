Saa Khao Griap

Rice Cracker Salad

ส้าข้าวเกรียบ Northern Thailand
Salad Rice

The “Saa Khao Griap” ( ส้าข้าวเกรียบ ) is a somewhat unusual salad from northern Thailand, which is prepared from soaked rice paper . This is mixed with black sesame seeds, snake beans, chilli paste and fish sauce. The salad is also known as “Yum Khao Griap” ( ยำข้าวเกรียบ ) or “Yum Khao Khaep” ( ยำข้าวแคบ ).
