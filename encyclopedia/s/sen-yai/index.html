Sen Yai

Fresh, Wide Rice Noodles

เส้นใหญ่ Central Thailand
Noodles

“Sen Yai” ( เส้นใหญ่ ) refers to very wide rice noodles in Thailand that are made from rice flour and tapioca starch. They are often used in wok dishes like Pad See Ew or Rad Na or also in noodle soups. The noodles have a soft but also slightly chewy texture and absorb the flavor of sauces very well.

Name
Sen Yai
Sen Yai (เส้นใหญ่)
Wide rice noodles, Rice sheets
Usages
  • Pad See Ew Sen Yai
  • Sen Yai Rad Na
  • Sen Yai Yentafo
Sen Yai can also be made from rice paper as a good approximation. I have documented how to do this in the article Thai Sen Yai from Rice Paper (reiskoch.de).
