Suea Rong Hai

Grilled Steak or Crying Tiger

เสือร้องไห้ Northeastern Thailand
Beef Grilled

“Suea Rong Hai” ( เสือร้องไห้ ) or “crying tiger” is a classic street food dish from Isaan. Grilled beef steak is served with a spicy sauce for dipping. There are many theories about the origin of the strange name, but none of them have been proven . The dish can also sometimes be found under the longer name “Nuea Yang Suea Rong Hai” ( เนื้อย่างเสือร้องไห้ ).

Name

Suea (เสือ)
Tiger
Rong (ร้อง)
