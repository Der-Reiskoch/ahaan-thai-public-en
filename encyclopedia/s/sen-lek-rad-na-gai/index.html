Sen Lek Rad Na Gai

Rice Noodles with Thick Sauce

เส้นเล็กราดหน้าไก่ Central Thailand
Noodles Chicken Stir Fried Gravy

“Sen Lek Rad Na Gai” ( เส้นเล็กราดหน้าไก่ ) is a noodle dish made with thin rice noodles and chicken. The ingredients are cooked in a sauce made from yellow bean paste, fish sauce, soy sauce, and oyster sauce, that is thickened with cornstarch. Depending on the recipe, the dish can have either a thick sauce or a soup-like consistency.
