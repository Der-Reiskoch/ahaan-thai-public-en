Yum Ruam Mit

Spicy mixed salad

ยำรวมมิตร Central Thailand
Salad Noodles Glass Noodles Seafood

In some sources, “Yum Ruam Mit” ( ยำรวมมิตร ) or “Yum Ruam” for short ( ยำรวม ) is a Thai salad with glass noodles and many other ingredients such as prawns, squid, minced meat, Vietnamese sausage and peanuts. This salad is dressed with a mixture of lime juice, fish sauce, chilies and palm sugar.
