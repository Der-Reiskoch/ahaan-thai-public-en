Khai Pad Thaeng Gwa Sai Moo

Stir-fried Egg with Cucumber and Pork

ไข่ผัดแตงกวาใส่หมู Central Thailand
Eggs Cucumber Pork Stir Fried

"Khai Pad Thaeng Gwa Sai Moo" ( ไข่ผัดแตงกวาใส่หมู ) is a quick stir-fry dish where pork belly is fried first, followed by cucumbers. Both are then mixed with beaten eggs seasoned with fish sauce, a little palm sugar, and white pepper. Once the eggs are set, the dish is served with jasmine rice. It is also known as “Pad Thaeng Gwa Sai Moo Sam Chan” ( ผัดแตงกวาใส่หมูสามชั้น ).
