Khao Pad Gaeng Phed Plaa Tuna

Fried Rice With Curry Paste and Tuna

ข้าวผัดแกงเผ็ดปลาทูน่า Central Thailand
Stir Fried Rice Fish

“Khao Pad Gaeng Phed Plaa Tuna” ( ข้าวผัดแกงเผ็ดปลาทูน่า ) is fried rice with canned tuna. It is seasoned with red curry paste, garlic, light soy sauce and fish sauce. Depending on the recipe, some coconut milk is also added. Vegetables such as green beans or baby corn can be used. The dish can also be found under the name “Khao Pad Prik Plaa Tuna” ( ข้าวผัดพริกปลาทูน่า ).
