Krueang Gaeng Som

Paste for Sour Curry

เครื่องแกงส้ม Central Thailand, Southern Thailand
Curry Paste

“Krueang Gaeng Som” ( เครื่องแกงส้ม ) is a paste for sour curries from central Thailand. It is prepared from dried chilis, white pepper, shallots, garlic, fingerroot and shrimp paste. The paste gives curries a reddish color and pleasant heat. It is also called “Prik Gaeng Som” ( พริกแกงส้ม ) or “Nam Prik Gaeng Som” ( น้ำพริกแกงส้ม ).
