Khao Yum Ko Yo

Rice Salad from Songkhla Lake

ข้าวยำเกาะยอ Southern Thailand
Salad Rice Seafood

“Khao Yum Ko Yo” ( ข้าวยำเกาะยอ ) is a rice salad from Ko Yo Island in Songkhla Lake in southern Thailand. The rice is mixed with various fresh herbs, vegetables, rice noodles in coconut milk, prawns, chilli powder and coconut flakes, and dressed with a dressing made from fish sauce or Budu, galangal, palm sugar, lemongrass and kaffir lime leaves.
