Krueang Gaeng Panaeng

Panaeng Curry Paste

เครื่องแกงพะแนง Southern Thailand
Curry Paste

“Krueang Gaeng Panaeng” ( เครื่องแกงพะแนง ) is the Thai name for panaeng curry paste. It consists of dried chili peppers, coriander seeds, cumin, lemongrass, galangal and roasted peanuts, among other things. This paste gives panaeng curry its characteristic thick consistency and mild spiciness. Other names for the curry paste are “Prik Gaeng Panaeng” ( พริกแกงพะแนง ) or “Nam Prik Gaeng Panaeng” ( น้ำพริกแกงพะแนง ).
