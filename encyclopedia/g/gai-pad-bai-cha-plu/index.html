Gai Pad Bai Cha Plu

Fried Chicken with Wild Betel Leaves

ไก่ผัดใบชะพลู Central Thailand
Chicken Stir Fried Pepper Leaves

“Gai Pad Bai Cha Plu” ( ไก่ผัดใบชะพลู ) is a stir-fry dish made with chicken that is cooked together with garlic and chilis. The chicken is then mixed with wild betel leaves that have been cut into strips and deglazed with fish and soy sauce. Depending on the recipe, the chicken can also be mixed with red curry paste during cooking. The dish is also known by the shortened name “Gai Pad Cha Plu” ( ไก่ผัดชะพลู ).
