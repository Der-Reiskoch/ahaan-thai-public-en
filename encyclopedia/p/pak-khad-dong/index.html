Pak Khad Dong

Pickled Mustard Greens

ผักกาดดอง Northern Thailand
Vegetables Fermented

"Pak Khad Dong" ( ผักกาดดอง ) is mustard cabbage that is pickled in brine with garlic and sometimes other spices. During fermentation, it develops a sour aroma similar to our sauerkraut or Korean kimchi. Pickled mustard greens are mainly used in northern Thai cuisine as a side dish with pork dishes, soups, or as an ingredient in wok dishes. This side dish is also known by its shortened name “Pak Dong” ( ผักดอง ).
