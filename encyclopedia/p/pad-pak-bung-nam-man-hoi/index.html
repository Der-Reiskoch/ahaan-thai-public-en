Pad Pak Bung Nam Man Hoi

Stir-Fried Water Spinach With Oyster Sauce

ผัดผักบุ้งน้ำมันหอย Central Thailand
Stir Fried Water Spinach

“Pad Pak Bung Nam Man Hoi” ( ผัดผักบุ้งน้ำมันหอย ) is a fairly simple wok dish. Here, water spinach is stir-fried with garlic and then finished with a mixture of oyster sauce and soy sauce. There is also a longer name for this dish: “Pak Bung Ya Leng Nam Man Hoi” ( ผักบุ้งยเลงน้ำมันหอย ).
