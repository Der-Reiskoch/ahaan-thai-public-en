Southern Thailand (Page 2)

Southern Thailand is a long, narrow strip of land whose cuisine is determined by the two seas that surround it. The cuisine there features many influences from neighboring Malaysia. Chinese immigrants who have lived there for generations have also shaped the cuisine.
